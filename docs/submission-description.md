# Zelyo — Submission Description

Zelyo is a privacy-preserving credentials protocol on Stellar that lets anyone prove a single fact about themselves — "I'm a certified Data Engineer," "I'm KYC-verified," "I'm over 18" — without revealing the underlying document or their identity. Today, proving you're qualified is all-or-nothing: you hand over a full diploma, transcript, or ID, and every platform that checks it becomes another honeypot of personal data waiting to be breached. Zelyo replaces "show me everything" with "here is cryptographic proof of exactly one fact." An issuer seals a credential and anchors it on Stellar; the holder generates a zero-knowledge proof in their own browser and discloses only what an opportunity requires; a verifier confirms it instantly. The only things ever written on-chain are a one-time, unlinkable stamp and the holder's wallet address — never a name, never a document, never a secret. For Stellar, this adds a missing primitive: a privacy-preserving identity and reputation layer that makes bot-proof airdrops, reusable KYC for anchors, and private eligibility checks for DeFi and DAOs possible — putting Soroban's zero-knowledge capabilities to work well beyond payments. The timing is ideal: Stellar's 2026 protocol upgrades shipped exactly the cryptography this needs, turning on-chain verification from aspiration into a supported building block.

**For reviewers:** the core experience is a four-step journey — an issuer seals a credential, the holder proves one fact about it in their browser, a verifier confirms the proof, and a gated job board releases a Stellar-native reward — with a live "nothing personal on-chain" panel and a Sybil-block demo (the same credential can't be claimed twice). We recommend walking that path end to end and inspecting any resulting transaction on a public Stellar explorer to confirm no personal data appears. Zelyo is an early-stage, hackathon-built project: the full seal → prove → verify → claim journey works today, while deeper capabilities — fully on-chain verification, multi-attribute disclosure, and reusable-KYC integrations with Stellar anchors — are on a clearly scoped roadmap. We deliberately prioritized one honest, working privacy guarantee over a broad but shallow feature list. We're glad to walk you through the vision, the Stellar integration plan, and the go-to-market (starting with the Philippines' remittance and freelancer economy, then APAC, then global). Thank you for reviewing — questions and feedback are very welcome.
