# Zelyo Pitch Deck — Image Generation Prompts

Prompts for the full-slide visual hooks in `pitch-deck-draft-Zelyo.pptx`.
Each corresponds to a `[FULL-SLIDE IMAGE — see image prompt #N]` placeholder in the deck.

---

## Prompt #1 — Title / hero (Slide 1)

A cinematic, full-bleed hero image on a warm cream paper-textured background. Centered focal point: a wax foil-stamp seal pressed into thick paper, embossed with a minimal abstract geometric mark (suggesting a lock or shield). Soft directional light rakes across the surface to catch the embossing and paper grain. Muted, premium palette — warm ivory, deep ink-navy, a single hit of gold foil. Lots of negative space around the seal for overlaid title text. Mood: trustworthy, official yet modern, quietly high-stakes. Style: editorial product photography meets fintech branding, shallow depth of field, no text in the image.

---

## Prompt #2 — Problem / contrast (Slide 2)

A full-bleed split-composition conceptual image contrasting two states. Left half (desaturated, anxious, cluttered): a messy pile of exposed personal documents — a diploma, an ID card, a transcript — with sensitive fields visible and red redaction lines, papers overlapping and spilling, cool grey lighting suggesting exposure and risk. Right half (calm, clean, confident): a single sealed envelope or wax-stamped card on a warm minimal background, showing only one small checkmarked label ("track ✓"), soft focused light. A subtle vertical seam divides the two worlds. Mood: from vulnerability to control. Style: clean editorial illustration / conceptual photography hybrid, restrained palette, no readable personal text on the documents.
